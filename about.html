About

What is Team Roulette?

Team Roulette is a free tool for randomizing teams in League of Legends custom games. Add your friends' summoner names, hit randomize, and get balanced teams in seconds.

Built for groups of friends who want to keep custom games fresh, fair, and fun.

Features

  • Randomly split any number of players into two teams
  • Assign random champions from the latest patch via Riot Data Dragon
  • Generate off-meta builds with items and runes
  • Assign lanes (Top, Jungle, Mid, Bot, Support)
  • Assign random summoner spells
  • Champion ban system with live swapping
  • Per-player reroll buttons
  • Quick Draft mode for 5-player lobbies
  • Wheel of Fortune spin mode
  • Random team names inspired by League lore
  • Save and load team presets
  • Share results via link

How It Works

Team Roulette uses Riot Games' Data Dragon CDN to fetch the latest champion, item, rune, and summoner spell data. Everything runs in your browser with no accounts, no backend, and no data collection.

Team presets are saved locally in your browser's storage.
